SERVICES

We offer 24/7 onboard supply service in order to completely meet your demands and provide your vessels with wide range of items & technical services. We work hard to deliver the best quality at competitive price.

We are proud of our industrial specialization in marine products and strong supply chain management capabilities. Kaan Marine Supply is striving to provide efficient and excellent service to any vessel calling ports in China.

Through complete pre-sale service, Kaan Marine makes quotations based on the inquiry together with sufficient communication on specifications / requirements and detailed remarks. We guarantee 100% delivery of products and quantity to the vessels. We promise 7 days, 24 hours response and always delivery on time.
